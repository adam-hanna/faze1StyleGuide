Typography

Font Choice

Open Sans is the official font of Faze1 and should be used in all marketing materials. However, if an alternate font is necessary to preserve a good user experience, Helvetica is the first alternate option and Arial is the second. These alternate fonts may be required for website or email content, where an individual’s computer settings may not support Open Sans.

Headlines

Headlines may be all caps or sentence case. Shorter headlines with 3-4 words in all caps show more emphasis, while longer headlines with more than 5 words in sentence case is preferred because it improves readability.
